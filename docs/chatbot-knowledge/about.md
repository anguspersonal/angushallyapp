> Source: `/about`

Angus describes himself as **"a startup operator and amateur developer with
a passion for the intersection of business strategy, software, and data."**

**Current role.** Angus is **COO and co-founder of [HeyLina](https://heylina.ai)**.
He frames the role as "a data strategist with a builder's bias and an
operator's discipline. A decade across Accenture and Anmut gives me the
framework behind our longitudinal emotional data play." At HeyLina he:

- built the marketing site, the internal operating system, and the
  engineering process around the mobile developer
- runs app-store launch operations, the interim raise, clinical advisor
  relationships, compliance, and pricing
- frames his partnership with co-founder Bri as: "Bri makes the company
  exist; I make sure it compounds"

He calls HeyLina "the most exciting thing I have ever worked on."

**Career history (most recent first):**

- **Anmut** — Data Strategy Manager. Worked on data valuation and data
  maturity tools like Grace.
- **Accenture** — started as an analyst on digital-transformation
  projects across the Royal Navy, the Police, and the Courts and
  Tribunals Judiciary (CTJ). Later moved into Accenture's strategy
  division, working on pricing, GDPR, and data-driven insights in large
  telecom and insurance companies.
- **TeachFirst** — mathematics teacher before all of the above. In his
  words: "to this day, the hardest thing I've done."

**This website.** Angus describes angushally.com as his "sandbox, a space
to explore personal software projects and challenge myself to put what I
learn and my thoughts out into the world." He frames the underlying intent
as: "A person honestly documenting their one shot at life. What they're
building, what they've learned, and how they think. Hope others will find
it useful now, or at least, something for my kids to look back on in
future."

The page closes with a contact-form CTA and the line "Thanks for stopping
by. I appreciate it."
